Web as Cityscape

The web is a bustling cityscape, with each page representing a unique building that contributes to the city's overall character and functionality. The home page is the central plaza, a bustling hub that guides visitors to various areas dedicated to different purposes. The navigation menu is a complex network of streets and avenues that guide users through the city's diverse services. Each page is a unique building, whether it's an information-rich skyscraper, an interactive museum or a bustling marketplace. Hyperlinks are fast vehicles that transport users seamlessly between different city blocks. The design of cities is dynamic and can grow and transform as new buildings appear and old ones are updated or removed. Just as cities shape the experience of their residents, websites shape their visitors' online journeys.
